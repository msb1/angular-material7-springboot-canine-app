About Cool Dogs of Notoriety

A Place for Dog Lovers of Every Breed

  • This website is dedicated to Man's Best Friend - The Doggie. It is not our objective to favor one breed over another as this is completely a matter of personal preference. Like the AKC and other Dog-Friendly Organizations, we encourage dogs as family companions; advance canine health and well-being; advocate for dog owner rights; and educate the public about responsible dog ownership. We are passionate about everything related to dogs, and we celebrate the joy they bring to our lives every single day. Being a dog lover is not a pastime; it’s a way of life – and we make every moment count. We cherish each day we spend with our beloved friends...

  • We at Cool Dogs of Notoriety are committed to the health, safety and well being of our canine friends.

  • At Cool Dogs of Notoriety, we are not partial towards one breed or another. We understand that all canine pets are special and shoudl be treated that way.

  • It is the hope from all of us at Cool Canines of Notoriety that everyone can find that special caninefriend to enrich their lives and provide companionship in a highly dysfunctional world.

  • This website is dedicated to two very special canine friends: Vyra "Noodles" and Frosty "The Bear".

  • Thank you for visiting our website.
